All About Poop!

Welcome to wonderful world of poop! No, really, this page is all about poop! You might think poop is pretty gross, but it’s actually very important. Let’s learn why.

Poop is Great

Well, it’s not great if we have to clean it up. But poop is really useful. Farmers use poop as food for plants. Without poop, we wouldn’t have those delicious tomatoes and cucumbers.

Poop is Delicious

Yes, poop is good food for all kinds of animals! Some animals eat their own poop. Haven’t you ever seen a rabbit or a dog eat their own poop? And a lot of insects eat poop, too.

Poop is Scientific

Poop tells us a lot of things. Poop can tell us about the food we eat and about our health. We can study poop from a long time ago to learn about people from long ago.

Poop is Amazing

Remember, poop might seem silly, but it’s also super important! So the next time you go to the bathroom, think about how amazing your poop is!
